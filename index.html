Hi, my name's Alistair. I lead the User Centred Design team at PDR. My work involves translating user insight and human factors testing data into design outcomes for our clients, with a particular focus on prototyping. This work includes products, services, interfaces and combinations of these. I'm also doing a part time doctorate in service design prototyping.
